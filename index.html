Kozani

Your perinatal health companion

Kozani is an AI-powered mental health companion for expectant and new mothers, especially in under-resourced communities. It provides trusted information and emotional support during pregnancy and early motherhood, without replacing professional care. Conversations are handled with care and privacy in mind.

If you ever feel unsafe, overwhelmed, or have thoughts of harming yourself, please reach out for immediate support. You can contact The Parent Centre on 021 762 0116 for confidential help.

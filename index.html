Kozani

Your caring companion through pregnancy and new motherhood

Pregnancy and early motherhood can feel joyful, overwhelming, beautiful, and exhausting. Sometimes all at once. You don't have to go through it all on your own.

Kozani is a friendly AI companion designed to support your mental and emotional well-being during pregnancy and the early months of motherhood, especially if you don't always have easy access to support. Kozani is here to listen, encourage you, and share simple, trustworthy information when you need it, without judgment.

Think of Kozani as someone you can check in with at any time, whether you're feeling anxious, low, unsure, or just need a moment to breathe and talk things through. It's a safe space to talk about what you're feeling.

What Kozani isn't

Kozani isn't a doctor or therapist and doesn't replace professional care. But it can help you feel heard and guide you towards extra support if you need it.

Your privacy matters

Your conversations are treated with care and respect and won't be shared without your permission. This is your safe space to speak honestly.

Speaking your language

Speaking your language Kozani can chat in several South African languages and is still learning, so thank you for your patience as it grows. We're working hard to help Kozani become more fluent in African languages. If something doesn't sound quite right, you're welcome to correct it or share feedback. That helps Kozani learn and serve moms better.

If you need urgent support

If you ever feel unsafe, extremely overwhelmed, or have thoughts of harming yourself or your baby, please reach out for real-world support. In South Africa, you can contact The Parent Centre on 021 762 0116 for confidential help.

You deserve care, compassion, and support, especially during this season of your life.
